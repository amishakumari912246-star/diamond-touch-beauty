How It Began

Born from a Passion
for Pure Beauty

Diamond Touch Beauty was born in 2020 when founder Amisha Verma, a botanist and skincare enthusiast, became frustrated with the beauty industry's reliance on synthetic chemicals. She began crafting formulas in her Mumbai kitchen, blending rare plant extracts sourced from across India.

What started as jars of serum for close friends quickly grew into a movement. Today, Diamond Touch Beauty serves over 50,000 women across India, with a simple promise: luxury beauty should be clean, kind, and effective.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How long does shipping take?
Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Express (1-2 days) is available at checkout. Free shipping on all orders above ₹999.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. Contact our support team with your order number and we'll arrange a pickup and full refund.
Are products suitable for sensitive skin?
Yes! All products are dermatologist-tested and free from harsh chemicals. We have a dedicated Sensitive Skin range. Patch test recommended for first-time users.
Do you ship internationally?
Currently we ship across India. International shipping to UAE, Singapore and the UK is launching in Q3 2025. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified!
Are all products vegan?
The majority of our products are 100% vegan. Products that are vegan certified are clearly marked on the product page with a vegan badge.
How do I track my order?
After placing your order, you'll receive a tracking link via email and SMS within 24 hours. You can also track your order from your account dashboard.
